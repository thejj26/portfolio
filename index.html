About Me

I'm Jakov JoziÄ‡, a 3rd grade student at the School of Electrical Engineering in Split, Croatia. I've had a passion towards programming since I was young. It started as some QBasic and Pyhton programming which was a part of my elementary school curriculum. I took some courses but later gave up on programming.

My love towards programming was reawakened when i took part in the EDIT Code School course in 2020. It was what got me into web development. After that I knew for sure that web development was the way for me.

Other than programming I dabble in a few other hobbies.

The newest addition to my life has been the wonderful world of custom mechanical keyboards. I've been following content regarding this topic for a couple of years but only got big into the hobby in 2022. I don't own a custom keyboard yet but I do hope that will change.

Another hobby I've been interested for as long as programming is electronics. While I'm not nearly as good in that field as I am in programming, I still like to create simple low power circuits and often try to expand my knowledge.

I mainly enjoy multiplayer FPS games such as Rainbow 6 SIege, Valorant, Overwatch and Paladins. I also enjoy playing singleplayer games of varying genres such as Minecraft, Wolfensten, Metal Gear Rising Revengeance and Doom.

I've also recentley grown interest into the audiophile world. I listen to music a fair amount and that has lead me to explore the world of high-end headphones.
